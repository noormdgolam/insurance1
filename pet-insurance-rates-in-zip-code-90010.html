Types of Pet Insurance Best for ZIP 90010

Given the area's high cost of living and increased risk of certain health issues, pet owners in ZIP 90010 may benefit from the following types of pet insurance:

  • Accident-only insurance: This type of insurance provides coverage for unexpected accidents, such as broken bones or poisoning.
  • Routine care insurance: This type of insurance provides coverage for routine veterinary care, such as vaccinations and dental care.
  • Wellness insurance: This type of insurance provides coverage for preventative care, such as flea and tick prevention and heartworm medication.
  • Comprehensive insurance: This type of insurance provides coverage for both accidents and illnesses, as well as routine care and preventative care.
