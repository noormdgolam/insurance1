How Much Is Pet Insurance in United Arab Emirates? A 2026 Guide

By Editorial Team | Last updated: July 11, 2026

Pet Insurance in the UAE: A Comprehensive Guide to Costs and Savings

Pet owners in the United Arab Emirates are taking proactive steps to ensure their furry friends receive the best medical care, regardless of the cost. With the average cost of pet care increasing, pet insurance has become a vital component in providing financial protection for pet owners. In this guide, we'll break down the costs of pet insurance in the UAE, factors that affect the premium, and valuable tips for saving.

Pricing and Average Costs

The cost of pet insurance in the UAE varies depending on several factors, including the pet's age, breed, and health conditions, as well as the policy's coverage and deductibles. Here are some approximate average costs of pet insurance in the UAE for popular pets:

  • Cat:** 1-2 years old: AED 800-AED 1,200 per year (pet owner pays out of pocket: AED 500-AED 1,000).
    • 5-7 years old: AED 1,200-AED 2,000 per year
    • 8-10 years old: AED 1,500-AED 2,500 per year
  • Dog:** 1-2 years old: AED 1,200-AED 2,000 per year
    • 5-7 years old: AED 2,000-AED 3,500 per year
    • 8-10 years old: AED 2,500-AED 4,500 per year
  • Other Pets (e.g., birds, aquarium fish):** AED 200-AED 500 per year

Factors Affecting Cost

The cost of pet insurance in the UAE is influenced by several factors, including:

  • Pet age and health:** Older pets and those with pre-existing medical conditions require more expensive policies.
  • Breed and genetics:** Some breeds are more prone to genetic disorders, increasing the cost of insurance.
  • Coverage and deductibles:** Higher coverage limits and lower deductibles result in more expensive premiums.
  • Pet's location:** Insurance costs vary depending on the UAE region, such as Abu Dhabi and Dubai.
  • Insurance provider:** Different insurance companies offer varying policies and prices.

Tips for Saving

To save on pet insurance in the UAE, consider the following:

  • Purchase a policy when your pet is younger:** Premiums decrease with age.
  • Choose a lower coverage limit or higher deductible:** Reduce your premium by accepting more out-of-pocket expenses.
  • Avoid policies with annual fees:** Some insurance companies charge annual fees alongside premiums.
  • Read the fine print:** Some policies include additional costs or restrictions.

Conclusion

Pet insurance is a crucial investment for pet owners in the UAE. By understanding the costs, factors affecting cost, and valuable savings tips, you can make informed decisions when choosing the right policy for your furry friend. Always research different insurance providers, carefully review policy details, and carefully consider your pet's specific needs to ensure you have adequate coverage at a price that fits your budget.
