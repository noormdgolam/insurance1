How Much Is Pet Insurance in New York? A 2026 Guide

By Editorial Team | Last updated: June 18, 2026

Pet insurance has become increasingly popular among pet owners in New York, providing financial protection against unexpected veterinary expenses. The cost of pet insurance in New York varies depending on several factors, including the type of pet, age, health, and coverage options. In this article, we will provide an in-depth analysis of the average monthly premiums, deductibles, breed-specific hereditary conditions, coverage options, and frequently asked questions about pet insurance in New York.

Average Monthly Premiums in New York

The average monthly premium for pet insurance in New York is around $50-$75 for dogs and $25-$40 for cats. However, these prices can vary depending on the insurance provider, coverage options, and the pet's age, health, and breed. Here are some examples of average monthly premiums for popular pet insurance providers in New York:

Provider Dog Premium (avg/month) Cat Premium (avg/month)
Healthy Paws $53 $29
Trupanion $64 $34
Nationwide Pet Insurance $58 $31
PetPlan $62 $36

Deductibles in New York

Deductibles are the amount of money you pay out-of-pocket before your pet insurance kicks in. The deductible for pet insurance in New York can range from $50 to $1,000, depending on the provider and coverage options. Here are some examples of deductibles for popular pet insurance providers in New York:

Provider Deductible (min/max)

Trupanion $50-$1,000
Nationwide Pet Insurance $50-$1,000
PetPlan $50-$1,000

Breed-Specific Hereditary Conditions

Some breeds are prone to specific hereditary conditions, which can affect the cost of pet insurance. For example, Bulldogs are prone to respiratory issues, while Poodles are prone to eye problems. Here are some breed-specific hereditary conditions and their associated costs:

  • Bulldogs: $500-$1,000 (respiratory issues)
  • Poodles: $300-$600 (eye problems)
  • Golden Retrievers: $200-$400 (cancer)
  • Labradors: $150-$300 (hip dysplasia)
  • German Shepherds: $100-$200 (hip dysplasia)

Coverage Options in New York

Pet insurance providers in New York offer various coverage options, including:

  • Accident-only coverage: Covers accidents and injuries, but not illnesses.
  • Wellness coverage: Covers routine care, such as vaccinations and check-ups.
  • Accident and illness coverage: Covers both accidents and illnesses.
  • Hereditary condition coverage: Covers breed-specific hereditary conditions.
